## 1.0 Purpose

This FAQ helps support reps answer common homeowner financing questions using approved language. Support must **not** provide tax, legal, or investment advice.

## 2.0 Financing Products Overview

SunGrid installations may be financed via:

| Product | Customer owns system? | Typical term | Notes |
|---------|----------------------|--------------|-------|
| **Solar loan** | Yes, after payoff | 10–25 years | Monthly payment to lender |
| **Lease (PPA-style)** | No — SunGrid/partner owns | 20–25 years | Payment tied to production or fixed fee |
| **Cash purchase** | Yes at install | N/A | No FIN-OPS payment queue |

Product type is shown in CRM under **Account → Financing Summary**.

## 3.0 Monthly Payment Questions

### 3.1 “Why is my payment different than I expected?”

Common causes (verify in CRM before answering):

- Escrow or autopay setup delay (first payment timing).  
- Insurance or property tax changes **unrelated** to solar (do not conflate).  
- Rate adjustments only if **variable-rate product** — rare; confirm contract.  
- Dual billing: customer pays utility **and** solar loan — solar reduces usage, not always total bill to zero.

**Support may say:** “Your solar loan payment is separate from your utility bill. Reduced electricity use may not eliminate your utility connection fee or nighttime usage charges.”

**Support may not say:** “Your payment is wrong and we will refund you” without FIN-OPS ticket.

### 3.2 Higher Utility Bill Despite Solar

Solar offsets **generation against usage**; it does not remove fixed utility charges, low-production months, or increased home consumption. Troubleshoot production separately via SG-TSG-2024-01 if customer believes system underperforms.

Escalate to FIN-OPS only if customer alleges billing error on **solar loan invoice**, not utility bill.

## 4.0 Lease vs. Ownership

### 4.1 Lease / PPA

- Customer **does not own** panels until buyout (if contract allows buyout).  
- Maintenance and warranty coordination often bundled — equipment issues still follow SG-WAR-2024-01 but ownership claims go through lease administrator.  
- Transfer on home sale requires lease assumption — FIN-OPS handles.

### 4.2 Loan / Cash Ownership

- Customer owns equipment subject to lender security interest until loan paid.  
- Warranty claims filed in customer name per SG-WAR-2024-01.  
- Customer may choose their own insurance endorsements — SunGrid does not advise on coverage.

### 4.3 Support Boundary

Explain **structural difference** between lease and own. Do **not** recommend which product should have been chosen.

## 5.0 Cancellation and Contract Exit

### 5.1 Cancellation Windows (General)

Cancellation rights depend on **state law** and **contract type**:

- **Rescission period:** Many states allow **3–14 business days** after signing **before installation** — exact dates in customer’s disclosure packet (CRM → Documents).  
- **After installation:** Cancellation generally **not available** on standard loans/leases; options may include sale of home, transfer, buyout, or payoff — **Financing Operations only**.

### 5.2 “Can I cancel after installation?”

**Approved response:**

> “After installation, your options depend on your specific loan or lease agreement and state rules. I can’t cancel contracts from support, but I can refer you to our Financing team to review what your agreement allows — such as transfer, buyout, or payoff.”

Open FIN-OPS ticket; do **not** quote cancellation fees from memory.

### 5.3 Cooling-Off Confusion

If customer believes they are still in rescission period, FIN-OPS must verify signature date and install status. Support collects dates only.

## 6.0 Tax Credit Disclaimer

SunGrid is **not a tax advisor**.

- **Federal Investment Tax Credit (ITC)** eligibility, amount, and timing depend on customer tax situation, income, and current IRS rules.  
- Support **must not** say: “You will get 30% back,” or “You qualify for the credit.”  
- **Approved:** “Many homeowners discuss the federal solar tax credit with their tax preparer. Your welcome packet describes how SunGrid documents installation for tax purposes, but we can’t provide tax advice.”

Direct all tax qualification questions to customer’s CPA and FIN-OPS for **document requests only**.

## 7.0 Billing Issues and Disputes

| Issue | Support action |
|-------|----------------|
| Wrong autopay bank account | FIN-OPS |
| Duplicate charge on loan bill | FIN-OPS — urgent tag |
| Request for payment deferral | FIN-OPS — hardship program |
| Utility bill dispute | Utility company — not SunGrid FIN-OPS |
| Production true-up (lease) | FIN-OPS with monitoring data |

Support creates FIN-OPS ticket with account ID, invoice dates, and customer description. SLA: **2 business days** callback.

## 8.0 When to Refer to Financing Team

Refer to **Financing Operations (FIN-OPS)** when customer asks about:

- Cancellation, payoff quote, or buyout amount  
- Payment amount disputes or deferral  
- Credit impact or reporting  
- Tax credit eligibility or amount  
- Lease transfer on home sale  
- Contract copies or amendment requests  

**Ticket template:** Category FIN → subcategory from table above → attach CRM financing summary → no speculative answers.
